About RP POP

Welcome to RP POP – Your Destination for Pure Music Vibes!

RP POP is a music-centric platform created with passion and dedication by Raj Pradhan. Designed for music lovers, artists, and dreamers, RP POP brings you the latest tracks, soulful melodies, and vibrant pop culture straight to your screen.

At RP POP, we believe music is not just sound — it's emotion, connection, and expression. Whether you're into mainstream pop, indie beats, or something in between, this platform is your digital stage.

Founded by Raj Pradhan, a music enthusiast and creative mind, RP POP is built to support fresh talent, promote musical creativity, and celebrate the power of rhythm in everyday life.

Join the vibe. Feel the beat. Welcome to RP POP.

Features:

  • Multi-language music library (Punjabi, Hindi, English)
  • Futuristic RGB lighting that changes with songs
  • Advanced search and playlist management
  • Sleek, responsive design for all devices
  • Regular updates with new tracks and features
